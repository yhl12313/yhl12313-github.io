TERMINOLOGY
Observation
Puzzles that require finding physical
clues, keys, symbols, or hidden objects
within the room set.
TIP. Search every corner, touch and examine everything
thoroughly, and don't forget high or low vantage points




Pattern
Challenfes based on identifying a
regular sequence in colors, shapes or
numbers to determine the next
step or code.
TIP. Focus on any repetitve visual or auditory elements,
and check if one set of information coresponds to the
order in a lock.
Deduction
Challenges that require combining
multiple pieces of information or using
deductive reasoning to infer the
correct answer.
TIP. Write down and visualize all clues, then use elimination
or look for relationships to logically deduce the solution.


Manipulation
Puzzles that involve physically moving,
assembling, balancing, or placing
objects in the correct way to trigger a
mechanism.
TIP. Examine all sides of the physical objects, and use
teamwork to manipulate pieces or devices simultaneously.
Encoding
Puzzles where letters, numbers, or
symbols are masked by a specific rule
and must be translated back.
TIP. Quickly identify the pattern or rule and try applying it
to the coded meffage.




Audio&visual
Puzzles that rely on sound cues or
visual effects to reveal information.
TIP. Immediately record audio or lioght patterns, and use
any available UV lights to scan the entire room for hidden
wirting.
